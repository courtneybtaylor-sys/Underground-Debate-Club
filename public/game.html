How to Play

1. Join the Lobby: Select a debate topic and choose your position (For/Against).

2. Prepare in Shadow Cabinet: Access three AI prep agents who generate 6 cards with research, rhetoric, and fact-check arguments.

3. Battle Your Opponent: Engage in 3 rounds of debate. Use power cards strategically (FLIP, FACT CHECK, MIRROR, AMPLIFY, RECESS, SILENCE).

4. Win the Voters: Sway 5 judges with Logic (40%), Rhetoric (30%), and Fact-checking (30%) to win 3 out of 5 voters.

5. Earn Influence: Win debates, build your streak, unlock the Oracle, and access the Underground network.
